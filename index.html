Your safety is our priority

Ridesharing with Rekab is a safe and affordable way to get around town. There are no surge prices, so you always know what you’re going to pay, and you can be sure that your driver has been screened and verified. Plus, thanks to the app’s rating system, you can be sure that you’re getting into a car with a safe driver.

Furthemore, Rekab cars are outfitted with cameras to ensure your safety. In the event of an accident or altercation, these cameras can provide vital evidence.

Special

Finally and in order to protect our passengers and staff from the risk of Covid19, we have taken some new measures on board. These include reducing the number of passengers in Rekab to a maximum of 3 people in addition to wearing an FFP2 mask.

This is how your order a ride

First, download the app and create an account. Then, enter your pickup location and destination. You can also select your preferred driver based on rating and reviews. Once you’ve chosen your driver, hit „request ride“ and wait for them to arrive.

Once the ride is over, payment is simple. Just enter your credit card information or PayPal account and your fare will be charged automatically.

You can also rate your driver and leave a review to help others make their decision about who to ride with.
